Destructions:
Enter a tabulated list above but use spaces instead of tabs.
Press GO to generate a tree diagram
Do not press go more than once as I have not written a reset method yet.
Use Shift + LMB to pan around the canvas.
Click and drag to select a number of nodes.
Once selected you can move the nodes by clicking on them and dragging.
Reset the position of a currently selected node by pressing 'r'.
To connect two nodes with an arrow, select one node, then hold shift and click on another node.
Create a new child node by selecting a node and pressing 'n'.
At the moment adding a new node will reposition all of the other nodes.
